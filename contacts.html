◉Creators

The developers of The Strongest Battlegrounds are the Roblox creator group Yielding Arts, a team known for producing high-quality combat games with smooth animations, detailed effects, and fast-paced gameplay. They launched the game in 2022, drawing heavy inspiration from anime-style battles, particularly characters and abilities similar to those seen in One-Punch Man. Over time, the developers continued improving the mechanics, adding new characters, movesets, and features while maintaining a competitive multiplayer environment.
